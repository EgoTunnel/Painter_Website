Keeping it in the family

It used to be that skilled trades were passed down from generation to generation, so much so that eventually the family would even carry the trade as their surname. While Marcus and Paul Belardi didn’t come from a family of painters, they are reviving the tradition of making business a family affair.

In 2014, the Belardi brothers joined forces to complete a massive project in downtown Toronto and have been working side by side ever since, as Belardi Painters.

We serve customers throughout the GTA. Send us a message and we'd be happy to provide you with an estimate.
